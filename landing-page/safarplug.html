Features

Safarplug EV Platform

Building EV charging infrastructure for India. Safarplug streamlines station discovery, simplifies route planning for cars and bikes, monitors live slot occupancy, and manages payouts for charging station owners effortlessly.

  • Track chargers in real time, view slots availability (AC, DC, DC Ultra-Fast).
  • Seamless UPI & Razorpay payment flow to book charging slots and settle invoices.
  • Map view integration containing user routes, nearby charger pins, and compatibility filters.
Discover More
Smart Discovery

Live Maps & Location Search

Locate nearby EV stations based on user coordinate radius. Filter chargers dynamically by vehicle compatibility (Car or Bike) and connector types (CCS2, Type 2, or standard plugs) on an interactive Google Map view.

Geo-Radius Detection

Scan nearby stations within a customizable radius (default 5.0 km) and retrieve address information automatically using reverse geocoding.

Slot Availability Markers

Map markers show live available connector counts. Active station statuses update in real time based on Firestore records.

Route Planner

Intelligent Trip Planner

Eliminate range anxiety on highways. Safarplug tracks your current battery percentage, total range, and plans the most optimal route with suggested charging stops along the way.

  • Auto-suggest stops based on EV range and starting battery levels.
  • Displays arrival battery percentage estimation, wait time, and target charging metrics.
  • Dead-zone warnings if a segment has no compatible stations nearby.
Live Session

Charging Session Progress

Monitor live charging statistics while plugged in. Users receive real-time updates on elapsed time, energy dispensed in kWh, total cost accumulation, and battery percentage metrics.

Real-Time Progress Streams

Live connection to Firestore keeps current battery percentage, estimated time remaining, and power levels (kW) updated every 30 seconds.

Automated Settlements

Ending the session triggers automatic calculation and billing based on the exact energy consumed, settled seamlessly using Razorpay gateway.

Monetization

Station Listing & Verification

Become a provider on the Safarplug network. Register your charging point by pinning your coordinates on the map, selecting power outputs, adding photos, and stating your per-kWh pricing.

  • Define custom charger slots (AC Slow 3.3kW to DC Ultra-Fast 150kW).
  • Select local amenities (Parking, Restroom, Waiting Area, Cafe) for user comfort.
  • Secure verification queue validates listed stations before making them active on the map.
Revenue Insights

Owner Dashboard & Payouts

Track today's revenue, dispensing metrics, and weekly trends from a clean telemetry control panel. Settle payouts directly to your linked bank account.

Dispensing Performance Charts

Graph daily energy consumption metrics and track earnings in real-time to adjust pricing strategies.

Charger Availability Toggles

Enable, disable, or mark specific charging slots offline directly from your dashboard to coordinate maintenance.

Data Security

Role-Level Access Isolation

Built on Firebase Auth and Firestore Security Rules. User profiles are isolated securely so EV drivers can only read their personal sessions and review details, while owners keep exclusive telemetry control.

  • Custom GoRouter guards prevent unauthorized screens access.
  • Storage rules ensure only verified accounts upload photo assets to station profiles.

Safarplug integrations to scale your EV Business

Telemetry

OCPP Smart Charger Sync

Synchronize smart hardware models with OCPP protocol compatibility. Settle connections instantly, send heartbeat alerts, and coordinate remote restarts securely.

  • Complete telemetry statistics sent directly to database dashboards.
  • Configure charger options, pricing structures, and connectivity status alerts.

Analytics

Analytics & Reports

View dispensing metrics, load management reports, and energy consumption history on a single page to evaluate return on investment.

  • Track daily charging operations, carbon savings, and station occupancy values.
  • Powerful graphs showing daily, weekly, and monthly utilization trends.
How It Works

Locate, Connect, and Settle in 3 Simple Steps!

STEP - 01
Locate Compatibility
Find compatible chargers (AC/DC) nearby or plan route stops directly using the Safarplug map locator.
STEP - 02
Plug In & Charge
Select your slot, initiate slot reservation payment, and plug in your electric car or bike. Track progress live.
STEP - 03
Auto Payout
Stop charging anytime. Final session costs settle automatically and payouts transfer directly to the owner.
