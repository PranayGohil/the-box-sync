Hotel Property Management

Manage guest stays, automate staff workflows, and optimize room utilization from a single platform. TheBox Property Management System (PMS) simplifies front desk reservations, guest check-ins, room shifting, and daily night audits to ensure smooth operations.

  • Track active bookings, room statuses, and category occupancy in real time.
  • Coordinate housekeeping schedules and maintenance statuses instantly.
  • Generate tax invoices (GST), manage refunds, and track payment transactions.

Reservation & Booking Management

Eliminate double bookings and manual calendar tracking. TheBox handles room availability dynamically, checking date overlaps and validating room statuses before confirmation. Manage corporate accounts, travel agents, and group rates seamlessly.

Overlapping Date Checks

Ensure room availability integrity with automated date and room ID overlap validations to prevent scheduling conflicts.

Corporate & Agent Portals

Directly input, track, and manage reservations from corporate partners, group booking requests, and travel agents.

Smart Allocation

Room Assignment & Shifting

Assign guest rooms dynamically based on category, floor, or availability. TheBox allows front desk staff to shift rooms or block specific rooms for maintenance without losing historical booking details or disrupting active guest stays.

  • Auto-assign vacant clean rooms based on guest category preferences and requests.
  • Block rooms for scheduled maintenance or deep cleaning without losing upcoming queues.
Front Desk Sync

Check-In & Check-Out Workflows

Synchronize reception and operations teams. Front desk managers can monitor incoming arrivals, execute fast check-in verification sheets, and compute accurate check-out balances based on room category tariffs and stay durations.

Fast Reception Desk

Review check-in status, verify guest IDs, and assign active room keys instantly from a single interactive desk dashboard.

Precise Tariff Calculations

Auto-calculate stay durations, apply correct room rates, and sum up room service additions upon guest check-out.

Room Status Sync

Housekeeping & Maintenance

Keep your rooms guest-ready. Housekeeping teams receive real-time room cleaning lists. Front desk staff can view room status updates (Dirty, Clean, In-Service, Maintenance) to prevent assigning uncleaned rooms to guests.

  • Auto-alert housekeeping teams of check-outs to trigger priority room cleanings.
  • Set maintenance blocks for AC, plumbing, or electrical repairs with assigned work orders.
Accurate Invoicing

Billing, GST & Refund Processing

Simplify payment reconciliations. TheBox computes room charges, applies tax parameters (CGST/SGST), and supports discount overrides or waiver allocations. Process payment refunds safely with full transaction audits.

Corporate Folio & Billing

Generate centralized invoices for corporate bookings, split bills between companies, and manage direct guest folios.

Secure Refund Flows

Process payment refunds with instant path-routing verification, keeping audit trails clean and preventing duplicate entries.

Security & HR Sync

Biometric Attendance & Security

Ensure high security and accurate staff tracking. TheBox integrates biometric face encoding verification directly in the staff panel, syncing logs with shift calendars and employee payroll profiles.

  • Secure biometric check-in/out and shift punches to eliminate buddy punching.
  • Restrict access to facial encodings database and administrative controls with strict permission checking.
Daily Closings

Night Audits & Daily Reconciliation

Execute night audits at the end of each day to reconcile room statuses, compile daily cash/card transactions, calculate outstanding guest ledger balances, and audit occupied logs for management review.

Occupied Logs Audit

Verify daily room statuses (occupied, clean, dirty) against active reservations to track actual usage.

Financial Balance Ledgers

Close the day's transaction logs and generate audit worksheets detailing daily collections, pending tabs, and taxes.
